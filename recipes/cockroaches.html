Coconut Ginger Cockroaches

Would you like to know how to make Coconut Ginger Cockroaches? I will tell you. But first, cup my genitals in your hands, they look so soft. So soft.

Steps

  1. Leave food out overnight for a week until there is a healthy cockroach colony.
  2. Befriend cockroaches. Give them each a name.
  3. Spend years teaching them language and bonding with them.
  4. Get into a lover's fight and cook them, just chuck them in a pot with some ginger bits and coconut milk.

And now, for the secret.
